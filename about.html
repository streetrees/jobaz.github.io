JOBAZ is a certified Makeup Artist & Hair Stylist specialising in creative direction, consultation, styling and execution.

Her commercial work has been featured in REBEL Magazine, Soft Shoulders Productions, runways & advertisement for fashion designers. She has worked with private individuals & celebrities to prepare for stage performances, corporate shootings, special events & weddings.

She also provides education and workshops for individuals who are looking to expand their technical knowledge from shade matching all skin tones, contouring & highlighting and perfecting a smoky eye. She brings high quality product, best in class technique as well as a reliable, open and focused approach to all her work. She is passionate about providing high quality work and results with people of all ages, races and orientations.
